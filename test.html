1.Overview

This application process is for Canadians applying for a patent protection in Canada.


File a patent in another country

To get patent protection in other countries you can:

  • apply directly to the patent offices in the countries you choose
  • apply to multiple countries at once using the patent cooperation treaty (PCT)

Each country (or region) approves applications and grants patents according to their laws.

File a Canadian patent from another country

To get patent protection in Canada from another country:

  • apply directly to the patent offices in the countries you choose
  • apply to multiple countries at once using the patent cooperation treaty (PCT)

We approve applications and grants patents according to Canadian patent laws.


What is a patent?

A Canadian patent gives you the right to stop others from making, using or selling your invention in Canada for (a term of) 20 years.
